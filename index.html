About the Artist

A Passion for Timeless Moments

Demo Wedding Studio was born from a deep love for human connection and visual poetry. With a background in fine art and documentary filmmaking, we approach every wedding as a unique story waiting to unfold.

Based in India, we travel across the world — from intimate hilltop ceremonies to grand royal affairs — always with the same singular focus: capturing love in its most authentic form.

Everything You Need to Know

We recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October–February in India). Popular dates can fill up over a year ahead. Contact us early to check availability.
Absolutely! We love destination weddings and have shot across India and internationally. Travel and accommodation costs are billed separately, and we're always transparent about pricing. Drop us a message with your destination.
You'll receive a curated preview gallery of 20–30 edited images within 2 weeks. The full gallery (500–1000 images) is delivered within 6–8 weeks. Wedding films take 10–14 weeks due to the editing process.
We blend candid documentary photography with artistic portraiture. Our style is cinematic and emotive — we capture real moments while also crafting intentional, beautiful portraits that feel natural rather than stiff.
For weddings, we always work as a two-photographer team to ensure comprehensive coverage — different angles, candid moments, and key rituals are never missed. Additional videographers can be added to your package.
We don't provide unedited RAW files, as editing is an integral part of our creative process. Every delivered image is carefully color-graded and refined. We believe each image should be a finished work of art.
We offer customized packages to suit every need — from intimate ceremonies to grand multi-day affairs. Our packages typically start with a base package for single-day coverage and can be tailored with films, pre-wedding shoots, albums, and more.
